Leads you all the way

Find the best variety of styles that offer supreme comfort and longevity

Leeds Footwear is a renowned footwear brand in Kerala. We manufacture and supply a wide range of footwear, made from superior quality raw materials such as PU, EVA and PVC. We began our journey way back in 2002. Within no time our products have become synonymous with quality and durability. The range encompasses gents, ladies and kids collection. We have a well equipped manufacturing unit in Malappuram, Kerala. We are engaged in manufacturing and exporting superior quality footwear that adheres to international quality standards.

Style in every step.

Our range of footwear is appreciated by everyone for its design, color and comfort. These are comfortable, soft, light in weight and friendly with feet. We maintain the highest standard of workmanship in what ever we do.

Quality is our footmark.

We ensure the highest standard in everything we do from sourcing the raw materials to the delivery of the finished products. During the manufacturing process, we take utmost care to ensure that our products maintain top durability and reliability. We always take extra step to ensure maximum customer satisfaction.
